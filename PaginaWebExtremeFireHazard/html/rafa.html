You're hungry. Kill the gods. Make a sandwich out of their corpses.


Snackrifice is a top-down boss rush in which you play as a little frog awakened from hibernation by an insatiable hunger. Explore the world, solve puzzles and defeat the twelve divine beings that rule over it to satisfy your appetite.



This game was born as one of two potential prototypes for a final game development degree project, the other one being the reworked Iru's Dawn.

Snackrifice was selected to be developed into a full-fledged game, and at the moment roughly half of the game is completed and it has been shown in nationwide industry events such as the Mad Games Show. The demo containing the two first biomes and 6 different bossfights will be available soon on Steam and itch.io
