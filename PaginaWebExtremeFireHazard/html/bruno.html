IRU'S
DAWN

The prototype that forged Extreme Fire Hazard

A sci-fi roguelite about an omniscient parasite.

Save the planet Iru from its influence...

...or infect everything in your path.

Iru's Dawn started as a 2020 highschool project. Two of our members, Rafa and Bruno, developed an early concept as part of their Diploma. Here, Iru's Dawn was a top-down pixel art RPG.

Since then, the project has evolved and the team behind it has grown.

2024 came, and University with it. The team grew, and Extreme Fire Hazard was created as it is known today. As a final Game Development Degree project, they developed two potential prototypes. One was Snackrifice.

The other one was Iru's Dawn.

The project was refactored and redesigned, into what it is today:

A 2.5D roguelite shooter where you face the killer of the Universe.

Snackrifice got selected as final project that would be further developed. The team put Iru's Dawn aside for the time, and centered their efforts in developing Snackrifice as their first fully fleshed full scale project.

But the Iru's Dawn prototype still exists... waiting... as a promise of what will one day be...

And as a reminder of where we, Extreme Fire Hazard, have started our journey!
